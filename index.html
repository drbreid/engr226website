Dr. Derek Breid

Saint Vincent College

Faculty member and engineering educator dedicated to helping students understand the relationships between material structure, properties, processing, and performance.

Course Description

This course introduces the fundamental principles of materials engineering, including the structure, properties, processing, and performance of engineering materials. Students will explore metals, ceramics, polymers, and composites through practical engineering applications.

Emphasis is placed on material selection, failure analysis, and the relationship between microstructure and material behavior. The course provides a foundation for advanced study and professional engineering practice.
